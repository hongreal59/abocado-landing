3. Screen Time & Family Controls

Abocado uses Apple's Screen Time and Family Controls frameworks to enable user-defined app access restrictions.

Authorization is required and controlled by the user through iOS Settings.

We do not access, collect, or store detailed Screen Time usage data from other apps.

Screen Time permissions are used solely to enforce user-defined app access restrictions configured within Abocado.

Information obtained through the Family Controls framework is processed entirely on-device.

This data remains locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third-party services.

Revoking permission disables restriction features but does not affect other App functionality.
