Privacy Policy for Abocado

Your privacy matters to us

Introduction

Abocado ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Abocado (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you provide directly when using the App, including:

  • Goal Information: Titles, descriptions, completion status, reminder preferences, and streak data for goals you create
  • User Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, and customization options
  • Account Information: If you create an account or subscribe, we may collect limited information such as your name and email address, processed through third-party services

Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • App Interaction Events: Events related to interactions within the Abocado app itself, such as when app-blocking rules are activated or deactivated, screens are viewed, or features are used. This does not include activity, usage, or content from other apps on your device.
  • Usage Information: High-level information about how features within the App are used, such as goals created or completed and timer usage duration
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics, crash reports, and error logs

Information from Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: When you make in-app purchases or subscribe, RevenueCat processes payment information and subscription status. We receive information related to your subscription status and entitlements.
  • PostHog Analytics: We use PostHog to understand how users interact with the App. PostHog may collect anonymized or pseudonymized usage events and technical information related only to activity within the Abocado app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and Maintain the App: Deliver core functionality such as goal tracking, timers, and user-defined app access restrictions
  • Process Transactions: Manage subscriptions and in-app purchases through RevenueCat
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features, fix bugs, and optimize performance
  • Send Notifications: Deliver local notifications for goal reminders, timer completions, and other App-related alerts (with your permission)
  • Personalize the Experience: Customize features and content based on your usage within the App
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Meet legal requirements and respond to lawful requests

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Most user data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Goals and completion history
  • User-defined app access rules
  • Progress and statistics
  • Timer state and preferences

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable optional cloud-based features, if available.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat processes payment information and subscription-related data in accordance with their privacy practices.

More information: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

PostHog

We use PostHog for analytics to understand App usage and improve functionality. PostHog collects information related only to interactions within Abocado and does not collect data from other apps on your device.

More information: https://posthog.com/privacy

Apple Services

Abocado uses Apple's Screen Time and Family Controls frameworks to enable user-defined app access restrictions. Authorization is required and managed by the user through iOS system settings.

We do not access, collect, or store detailed Screen Time usage data from other apps. Screen Time permissions are used solely to enforce user-defined app access restrictions configured within Abocado.

Permissions and Access

Screen Time / Family Controls

Abocado requests Screen Time permission to allow users to manage access to selected apps based on user-defined rules configured in the App. This permission is optional and can be revoked at any time through iOS Settings. Revoking access will disable app restriction features but will not affect other core functionality.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications for:

  • Goal reminders
  • Timer completion alerts
  • Daily motivation messages

Notification preferences can be managed through iOS Settings.

Background App Refresh

Abocado may use background processing to maintain timer-related functionality and update App state. Disabling background app refresh may impact timer behavior when the App is not in the foreground.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers (such as RevenueCat and PostHog) solely to operate and improve the App
  • Legal Requirements: When required to comply with applicable laws or lawful requests
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View information stored locally through the App interface
  • Delete Your Data: Remove all App data by uninstalling the App or using in-App data clearing options, if available
  • Opt Out of Analytics: Request to opt out of analytics collection where applicable
  • Manage Permissions: Control App permissions through iOS Settings

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until deleted by you
  • Analytics Data: Retained by PostHog according to their retention policies
  • Subscription Data: Retained by RevenueCat as required for subscription management and compliance

Children's Privacy

Abocado is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

International Data Transfers

Information may be processed in countries outside your place of residence, including the United States or European Union, depending on the services used. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

California Privacy Rights

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to access and delete personal information. Abocado does not sell personal information.

European Privacy Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including access, correction, deletion, and restriction of processing.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date.

Additional Information

Data Minimization

We collect only information necessary to provide and improve the App. We do not collect sensitive data such as location, contacts, photos, media, or health information.

No Cross-App Tracking

Abocado does not track activity across other apps or websites. Analytics are limited to activity within the App itself.
